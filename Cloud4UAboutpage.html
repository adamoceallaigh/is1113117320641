Who We Are

Cloud 4 U are an internationally recognised cloud services vendor who cater for everyone's cloud servicing needs.
Through our time honoured traditions of honesty and diversity we have established a good client and customer based relationship,
which we cherish and strive to continue long into the future. Our services are delivered with 100% merchantable quality,
they are universally accepted as being fit for the purpose intended and continuosly correspond to the dedicated description.
Owing to our respectable reputation,we have been afforded many outstanding opportunities, servicing the big giants in the world of commerce.
Our headquarters are based in the hub of e-commerce and technology that is Silicon Valley.

Our mission is to sell software that allows easy organisation of the business' information and make it accessible and useful.
Since our founding in 1998, Cloud 4 U has grown by leaps and bounds.
From offering our services to small-scale backyard shops to thus now offering them to the technological giants of this world including Microsoft,
Apple, HTC,Coca Cola etc.




Whether it be a small business or a multinational, we've got Cloud 4 U.

What We do

We here at Cloud 4 U are a cloud service vendor, as that title entails we provide and offer the latest and greatest cloud computing softwares on the market to your everyday
business' and also your recognised tech giants of this world. As a cloud service vendor we analyse the market and decide upon the next step in cloud computing software and where it can be enhanced. We adopt a customer orientated approach
as to suit the needs of our clients to the most appropriate softwares for their business and we do regular checkups to ensure our software runs as smoothly as it should. We are
very dedicated to providing the best in the market in regards to cloud services to our clients as it is afterall, them who
keep funding our prosperous career in this field of trade and for this we are very appreciative. Our range of products are extremely diverse and we offer IAAS, SAAS, PAAS and CAAS alike.
