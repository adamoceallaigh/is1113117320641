FOOTBALL \ SOCCER

My interest in football \ soccer stems from an early age. When I was as young as 4 I inherited in my dad's passion for gaelic football, with him being the manager of almost 6 underage teams including my one,i had no choice really. My dad coached me in my backyard nearly everyday and i improved greatly as a result. I started taking a genuine interest in both sports when i discovered my passion for the Premier League.My favourite team was and is still Chelsea Football Club.I enjoy watching the premier league games on tv and I was privileged to have the opportunity to travel to Stamford Bridge to watch my team live. I played multiple underage games in both codes respectively and have achieved a few county championships in the process.
