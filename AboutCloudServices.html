About Cloud

What Is The Cloud  ?

Perhaps no computer term is more misunderstood than cloud computing. To break down the miscontrued concept we're going to use an analogy everyone is familiar with.



Utility companies store resources like water, electricity, and natural gas so they can deliver them to you when you need them, cloud service providers do the same thing.



Cloud Computing resources (servers,storage, virtual machines,networks,hosted applications/web-services etc..) are stored in datacenters all around the world that you can access by simply signing up for an account and clicking a few buttons… all of this without requiring you to set up any computer hardware on your end then like the water that flows freely from your utility company into your tap, cloud providers deliver computing resources in the form of access to servers, storage, networks, and cloud applications through the “pipes” of the internet.




Everything "just works" without concern about the underlying infrastructure - simply pay for what you use.
